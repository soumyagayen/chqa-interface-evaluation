how to treat throat pain?
How is it treated? Doctors treat back pain with medications, other treatments, and surgery. Medications may help relieve pain or help tense muscles relax. In addition, your doctor may tell you to: Use cold packs to help lower some back pain and hot packs to increase blood flow and help you heal. Limit activities or exercise that cause pain or make it worse, but do not lay down all day.
